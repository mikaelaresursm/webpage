For assignment one, we were asked to design a 24” × 36” digital poster about your life under COVID 19 without using any software explicitly intended for design. I chose to dedicate my poster towards the people I lost to COVID-19 and also focus on how isolated my life now became with my everything being digitalized. For my final, I ended up using my printer to scan each screen shot from my phone individually, to showcase the most important times I checked my phone during those years.
